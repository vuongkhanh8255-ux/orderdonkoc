Privacy Policy

Last updated: [ngày cập nhật]

APPCASH CO., LTD (“we”, “us”, the “Company”), operating under the Stella Kinetics brand, respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website and our internal business tools.

1. Information we handle

For our internal analytics platform, we access advertising and performance data only from advertising accounts that we own and control (e.g. impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, CPC, ROAS). We do not collect personal data of end users through this tool, and we do not access accounts that do not belong to us.

2. How we use information

  • To measure and report on the performance of our own advertising campaigns;
  • To generate internal reports for our marketing team;
  • To optimize our advertising budgets and operations.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or share advertising data with third parties. Access is restricted to authorized employees of the Company for internal use only.

4. Data security

Access credentials and tokens are stored securely and used solely to retrieve reporting data for our own accounts. We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards.

5. Compliance

We comply with the platform policies of the marketplaces and advertising platforms we use, including TikTok and Shopee, and with applicable Vietnamese law.

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@[domain-mới].vn.
