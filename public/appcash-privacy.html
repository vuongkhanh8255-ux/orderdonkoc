Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

APPCASH CO., LTD ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website https://appcash.app and the Stella Kinetics e-commerce business. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our business operations and internal tools.

1. Information We Collect

We collect business and operational data necessary to run our e-commerce stores, including:

  • Order, product, and sales data from the e-commerce platforms we operate on (TikTok Shop, Shopee).
  • Advertising and campaign performance metrics from our own advertising accounts.
  • Account and authentication information for our internal team members who use our tools.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely for legitimate internal business purposes: monitoring sales and advertising performance, generating internal reports, managing inventory and orders, and improving our operations. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

3. Data From TikTok and Shopee APIs

We access data through official platform APIs (such as the TikTok Business API and Shopee Open Platform) strictly for our own authorized business accounts. This data is used only for internal analytics, reporting, and campaign optimization, and is handled in accordance with each platform's developer terms and policies.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and restricted internal access. Our tools are for internal use by authorized staff only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, rent, or sell collected data to third parties, except as required to operate our services (e.g., infrastructure providers) or to comply with applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy and to comply with legal obligations.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@appcash.app.
